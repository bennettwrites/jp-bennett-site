Shadow Kingpin: Knox’s War

From wildland fire to black ops: one man’s war in the shadows.

Once a pilot for the U.S. Forest Service, Knox now flies deeper into the world’s shadows as a covert operative for an agency so hidden it doesn’t officially exist. What began as quiet cargo flights and covert operations unravels fast when anomalies in flight manifests trigger an investigation.

Wishing for the simpler days of college weekends and backcountry landings, Knox is thrown into a high-stakes shadow war. Spy networks awaken. Old rivals resurface. Loyalties bend.
